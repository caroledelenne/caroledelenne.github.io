Mitra Aelami
======

*"Urban flood risk modeling using neural networks and the impact of extreme spatio-temporal rainfall events."*
**University of Montpellier**, 01/10/2024–2027.
**Supervisors**: V. Guinot, G. Toulemonde; **co-supervision**: C. Delenne and R. Hostache.
[theses.fr/s403144](https://theses.fr/s403144), [ORCID 0009-0003-2871-5649](https://orcid.org/0009-0003-2871-5649).
Hydraulic models will be applied at different scales to serve as a training basis for an artificial intelligence model enabling rapid estimation of flooded areas ([Aelami.etal2026_SHF](#), [Aelami.etal2026_RiverFlow](#)).
